Legal
Important notices

VPN/Proxy Disclaimer: ShieldBrowse provides proxy routing configuration. It does not operate or provide proxy/VPN servers. Users are responsible for choosing a trustworthy proxy provider and complying with local laws regarding VPN/proxy usage in their jurisdiction.

Ad Blocking: Ad blocking is legal in all major jurisdictions including the US, EU, UK, Canada, Australia, and India. ShieldBrowse does not modify website content on their servers — it controls how your browser renders content it has already received.

Open Source: ShieldBrowse is released under the MIT License. Source code is available for audit.
